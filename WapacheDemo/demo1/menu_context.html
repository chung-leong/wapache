I am a window with a custom context menu. Right click on the page elements to bring them up. The text selection menu will come up when you select some text and right click on it. My settings and the menu definitions are listed below:

www.google.com 
	
<StandardWindow demo_context>
	VerticalAlign Bottom
	HorizontalAlign Middle

	Height 50%
	Bottom 32px
	AnchorContextMenu a_menu
	ImageContextMenu img_menu
	DefaultContextMenu def_menu
	TextSelectionContextMenu sel_menu
	ControlContextMenu ctl_menu
</StandardWindow>
